Who Is Fox?

For most of my life, various forms of art have grabbed my attention and drew me to create things of my own. Whether or not I could be succesful in conducting business with my ideas, its rest assured that I'll be creating regardless due to my natural love for personal invention and exploration. Frankly, I just love creativity in general!

If I had to narrow down the particular arts I make & dwell on, my most frequented passions are Music Production, Graphic Design, & Lyricism! From tracks composed in Ableton Live 8, to logos and character designs both 2D & 3D, and lastly rap lyrics set to my beats which I write in Google Docs! As far as where I'd like to see myself creating in a career sense, to become a proclaimed rap lyricist & actor are surely major dreams of mine! And while not as often dabbled with as other fields, Photography also livens and inspires me greatly~

What I am striving to accomplish as an artisan is to create clearly inspired works, works that'd likely center around high energy/big attitude rhyme schemes, characters & locations belonged largely to horror narratives, and physical performances for said narratives.

While I'd love to demonstrate all I've created here, this page is hosted freely on GitHub Pages, so there's a clear "20 megabyte" cap for anything I choose to add in. That said, few examples of my Photography and Music Production will be shown off. Hope you enjoy! :>
